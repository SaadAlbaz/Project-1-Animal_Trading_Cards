Falcon

Falcons are used in falconry for at least 3000 years. They were very popular during the WWII due to ability to intercept pigeons that were carrying various messages.

  • Height: 9 to 19 inches
  • Weight: 1.5 to 2.6 pounds
  • Speed: 200 miles per hour
  • Lifespan: around 15 years

Falcons have appeared on the planet 8 to 24 million years ago and managed to adapt to the life in nearly all types of habitats. Unfortunately, as a result of habitat destruction, pesticide poisoning, illegal hunting and collecting of egg from the wild, some species of falcon are classified as vulnerable or endangered.
